Agriculture is the main source of the world food production. It covers 70 to 85% of the food supply. In the case of Africa agriculture with rainfall is not sufficient for food production because of prevalence of different factors that negatively affect growth. Environmental situation, deforestation and soil degradation are serious problems for development of rain-fed agriculture in the world especially in Africa

Irrigated area per capital is declining due to enlargement of population growth. Beside this the availability of water resources may not be able to meet various demands in the near future, but the inevitably more area to be brought under irrigation in order to achieve a sustainable global food security for the increasing population. These results will decline of land potential for irrigation by affecting the land suitability and reducing the water availability.
